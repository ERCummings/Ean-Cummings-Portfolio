About

My Story

I chose engineering because I wanted to create tools that help people live healthier lives. What started as an interest in medicine shifted when I realized I was more inspired by the devices that empower health — from wearables to apps. Today, I’m driven to design solutions that combine health tech with broader fields like renewable energy and IoT, making technology practical, human-focused, and impactful.

My Philosophy

I believe opportunity favors the bold — ideas move forward when you’re willing to test, iterate, and learn. As an engineer, I value curiosity, persistence, and collaboration as much as technical skill. My goal is to bring that mindset to every team I join, turning complex challenges into real-world solutions.
